Think Linkedin, but for startup founders and angel investors. What's the point of this if they can find eachother on Linkedin? Well, they can't.

Atlas, an ecosystem built for startup founders that can't find the angel investor hidden behind connections and the room's they can't get into and for angel investors that spend everyday having coffee chats trying to find their match.

With Atlas, a founder can upload their 60-second pitch to the Atlas feed that is manually and AI vetted. Investors can then scroll these pitches, including AI generated summaries, industry insights and thorough analysis using our AI-debate model.
